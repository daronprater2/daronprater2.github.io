Homewhere

The personalized recommendation system for living in Chicago

HomeWhere has two application Modes:

  • View By Metric - When in the view by metric mode, follow these steps:
    1. Select the Metric of interest from the preferences menu.
    2. Colors on the map indicate whether a zip code is "Good" or "Bad" for that metric.
    3. Hover over the zip codes to get all metrics on that zip code.
    4. Move the slider in the Time menu to see how the zip codes look into the future.
  • View By Total
    1. Move the sliders in each category based on your preferences.
    2. The farther right the slider indicates the more you care about the metric. For example, sliding Median Home Price all the way to the left indicates you aren't concerned about the home price for an area.
    3. Click refresh map. Green zipcodes indicate you would like living in that area and red zip codes indicate you wouldn't like living in that area.
    4. Move the slider in the Time menu to see how your preferred zip code would change in the future.

Color Scales for each Metric

  • Median Home Price
    1. Green: Low median home price
    2. Red: High median home price
  • Everyday Shopping
    1. Green: Lot's of shopping
    2. Red: Low shopping
  • Nightlife/Social/Entertainment
    1. Green: High volume of entertainment establishments
    2. Red: Low volume of entertainment establishments
  • Crime
    1. Green: Low crime rate
    2. Red: High Crime Rate
  • Education
    1. Green: High rated Schools
    2. Red: Low rated Schools
  • Park
    1. Green: High volume of parks
    2. Red: Low volume of parks
